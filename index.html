About Me

I’ve spent nearly 20 years in Customer Success across infrastructure, cloud-native SaaS, cybersecurity, and now AI exposure management. My belief is simple: software defines the speed of innovation — and companies that can’t operationalize and secure it will be disrupted by those who can.

My focus is turning complex technology into measurable customer outcomes.

I build trusted advisor relationships and design customer programs where proof drives growth. Risk reduction. Adoption milestones. Executive-level value narratives. Referenceable wins. I don’t rely on persuasion to drive renewal — I anchor it in documented impact.

Most of my work has been in early-stage and high-growth environments where the product is still evolving and process doesn’t exist yet. I’m comfortable building the first playbooks, QBR standards, and scalable frameworks while the company is still shipping fast. I create structure without bureaucracy and momentum without chaos. Some of my technology certifications are highlighted here.

Under pressure, I stay calm and anchor teams around the customer’s desired outcomes. Escalations get resolved by clarifying the “why,” aligning cross-functionally, and balancing customer urgency with business realities.

Strategically, I focus on deepening platform adoption, expanding enterprise footprint, and aligning Customer Success with emerging priorities like AI security. My growth edge is efficiency — eliminating operational friction so teams can scale impact faster.

At my core, I translate innovation into provable customer value — and turn that value into sustainable growth.
